About Defter

We're a collective of designers, architects, and creative thinkers crafting meaningful interior spaces.

At Defter Designs, we believe luxury is not about excess—it's about intention. Every material, every color, every line is carefully chosen to reflect the client's vision and enhance the quality of everyday life.

With over a decade of experience in luxury interior design, we've transformed homes and commercial spaces across Lagos and beyond. Our philosophy combines timeless elegance with functional innovation, ensuring every project becomes a reflection of our clients' aspirations.
